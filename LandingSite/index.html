About

Find Out More About Us

The purpose of this project is to make a software that manages the maintenance of industrial equipment.

General Information

This initiative aims to streamline and enhance the maintenance processes for industrial equipment, ensuring optimal functionality and longevity.

  • Project Scope

    It includes the design, development, and implementation of the Industry Equipments Maintenance System.

  • Product Perspective

    It seamlessly integrates into existing organizational structures, enhancing the efficiency of maintenance processes and contributing to the overall reliability of industrial machinery

Its compatibility with industry standards ensures seamless integration with other tools and technologies, further solidifying its position as an essential element in the lifecycle of industrial equipment.

Services

Check our Services

Here are some services that we offer to our clients, for sheduled maintenance of Equipments in their Industries.

Manage Company Info

You can manage the information of your company or factor, such as name, registration number, foundation date & Address.

Manage Representative Info

You can manage the information of your company's representative. That representative can be the owner, area manager, technical manager, director etc. Manage name, CNIC, joining date & email etc.

Specify the Industry

We have a list of common industries. You can specify which industry are you working in.

Manage Packages

We have different pricing packages available to choose from. You can change the package of you choice at any time.

Manage Equipments

You can manage (add, edit, list, delete) your equipments within the package range.

Set Shedule

You can set shedules of your equipments. Set it for maintenance every weekend, once in a month, either quarterly, twice a year, or once every year.
